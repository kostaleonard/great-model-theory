BinaryDifferentiableFunctionWithBroadcast

trait BinaryDifferentiableFunctionWithBroadcast[T] extends DifferentiableFunction[T]

A differentiable function with two arguments that broadcasts its operations.

This function broadcasts the results of its operations together based on the NDArray broadcasting rules. Its output shape is the result of this broadcast.

T

The array element type.
